Location

üìç Pollachi, Tamil Nadu

Situated in the foothills of the Western Ghats, one of the world's eight "hottest hotspots" of biological diversity. Our retreat is surrounded by thriving coconut and mango plantations, offering an authentic farm experience.

Access: Easily accessible from Coimbatore International Airport (40 km), with excellent connectivity to major South Indian cities including Chennai, Bangalore, Kochi, and Madurai.

Best Time to Visit: October to March for pleasant weather, or June to September for lush monsoon greenery and active wildlife.
